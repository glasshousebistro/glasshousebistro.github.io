Who We Are

LOCATED IN SUNNY CLEVELEYS, NEAR BLACKPOOL, A ONE MINUTE WALK FROM THE SEAFRONT. GLASSHOUSE IS A BRIGHT, AIRY COCKTAIL BAR AND BISTRO WITH A FULLY GLAZED EXTERIOR. SERVING A MIX OF UNIQUE GOURMET BURGERS & DELICIOUS STEAKS ALONG SIDE SPECIALITY AND CLASSIC COCKTAILS, WITH AN EVER CHANGING SPECIALS MENU. OUR STYLE IS RELAXED AND FRIENDLY, WITH AN INVITING COLLECTION OF LOCAL ART GRACING THE WALLS.


JOIN US FOR A COCKTAIL AND A BITE TO EAT WITH YOUR PARENT, SIBLING, PARTNER, BEST FRIEND, DOG (YES, WE ARE DOG FRIENDLY IN THE BAR ON A WEEKEND) - WE’D LOVE TO SAY ‘HI’.
